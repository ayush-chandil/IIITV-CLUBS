ABOUT US- Indian Institute Of Information Technology,Vadodara is an Institute of national importance established in 2013.Institute has various clubs of different fields.

Obscura is a photography club of IIIT VADODARA.It organises various events like Photowalk,Theme Photography events and others.
Masquerade is a Drama Club of IIIT VADODARA.Stage Performances,Mime all are organised and performed by them.
Coding Club Of IIIT VADODARA organises coding contests,interactive sessions on fields related to CSE and IT,Hackthons and many more.
Shades Club Of IIIT VADODARA makes beautiful sketches,portarit and other some amazing art work.
MOD 5,Dance Club of IIIT VADODARA performs various forms of dance in the institute and in various other institutes like DAIICT,IIT Gandhinagar.
Arcadia-Gaming Club of IIIT VADODARA organises pc and mobile games like PUBG,COD and many more.
DOT-Design Club of IIIT VADODARA organises various Design art work competitions.
Encore-Music Club of IIIT VADODARA sing song,orchestra on many festivals which are celebrated in institute.
ENIGMA-Mathematics club of IIIT VADODARA organise maths contests on different Topics of math like integration.
